SpaceCollab

About

SpaceCollab is an immersive collaboration platform that provides virtual spaces for remote training and collaborations.

The Challenge

The global pandemic has restricted our ability to connect emotionally and share knowledge due to limitations on in-person gatherings. Additionally, training can be expensive as it requires resources for space, equipment, and other costs for both trainers and participants. Moreover, access to rooms, machines, and tools necessary for human development is often limited, further hindering opportunities for growth and learning.

The Solution

We offer SpaceCollab, a virtual collaboration platform with several key features. Firstly, it enables multiplayer interaction, allowing multiple individuals to inhabit the same virtual space and engage with one another. Secondly, SpaceCollab is multiplatform, supporting various VR and non-VR devices, making it accessible to anyone. Thirdly, it provides true immersive presence through customizable avatars and spatial audio. Fourthly, it fosters interactivity by enabling users to engage with objects and other players within the virtual environment. Lastly, SpaceCollab is highly customizable, allowing users to easily tailor scenarios and incorporate additional objects according to their needs.
