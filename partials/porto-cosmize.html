COSMIZE

About

COSMIZE is a virtual social platform where you can talk to friends and participate in events. Here, people can use favorite avatars customized by yourself and enjoy the world of COSMIZE!

Not only chat with friends, you can also enjoy limited-time events held in special spaces in collaboration with famous brands and companies.


The Challenge

The Metaverse has skyrocketed in popularity, poised to become humanity's second world. It's not just an escape from reality; it's a canvas where people can fully express themselves and turn their wildest imaginations into tangible experiences within a vibrant community setting. We envision an ecosystem where individuals can harness the power of their second reality, empowered by blockchain technology to ensure transparency and trust. Our goal is to foster a space where creativity flourishes, enabling communities to craft their own digital goods and shape the Metaverse into a dynamic playground of endless possibilities.

Experiment: AI

In the evolving landscape of the metaverse, there is a need for intelligent conversational characters that can interact with users in a natural and engaging manner. Current chatbots and virtual assistants lack the depth and authenticity required to create immersive and meaningful interactions. Therefore, there is a demand for an AI-driven conversational character capable of understanding, responding, and adapting to user input in a dynamic metaverse environment.
